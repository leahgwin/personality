A Glance Into the Life of an ESTP

ESTPs keep their conversation energetic, with a good dose of intelligence, but they like to talk about what is – or better yet, to just go out and do it. Entrepreneurs leap before they look, fixing their mistakes as they go, rather than sitting idle, preparing contingencies and escape clauses.

Entrepreneurs are full of passion and energy, complemented by a rational, if sometimes distracted, mind. Inspiring, convincing and colorful, they are natural group leaders, pulling everyone along the path less traveled, bringing life and excitement everywhere they go. Putting these qualities to a constructive and rewarding end is Entrepreneurs’ true challenge.

How I Prefer Interacting and Communicating

  1. Written, clear instructions
  2. Everyone on the team sharing responsibilities and carrying their weight
  3. Taking time to plan out the project and delegate responsibilities
  4. Positive, direct communication
  5. Transparency
